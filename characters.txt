# Cornchip Day — Character Bible

## Cornchip (Protagonist / Playable Character)
- **Silhouette:** An upside-down triangle, corn-chip textured, with comedic articulated legs, expressive eyes, and a mouth.
- **Role:** Player character. Runs, jumps, and collects ingredients across 7 levels to win back his brother.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Plucky, determined, food-pun-driven optimist; treats every obstacle as a minor, laughable setback rather than a threat, consistent with the game's no-fail-state design philosophy.

## Wrap (Cornchip's Brother)
- **Silhouette:** A rounded, wrap/burrito-shaped body with comedic articulated legs, expressive eyes, and a mouth.
- **Role:** Cornchip's brother and the source of each level's obstacles — thrown pun ingredients ("mild" salsa, vegetarian cheese, etc.). Currently framed as the game's recurring antagonist / "main boss" figure.
- **Narrative Function:** Estranged from Cornchip after an argument. Reconciliation occurs once Cornchip has collected all 7 ingredients and presents them to make "the ultimate crunch wrap."
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Proud and stubborn on the surface; the obstacle-throwing reads as wounded-sibling antics rather than genuine villainy, and he wants to reconcile as much as Cornchip does.

## Cheeto (Post-MVP)
- **Role:** Cornchip's best friend; planned second playable character for a future local co-op mode.
- **Status:** Concept only — no visual design or mechanics required for MVP.
