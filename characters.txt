# Cornchip Day — Character Bible

## Cornchip (Protagonist / Playable Character)
- **Silhouette:** An upside-down triangle, corn-chip textured, with comedic articulated legs, expressive eyes, and a mouth.
- **Role:** Player character. Runs, jumps, and collects ingredients across 7 levels to win back his brother.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Plucky, determined, food-pun-driven optimist; treats every obstacle as a minor, laughable setback rather than a threat, consistent with the game's no-fail-state design philosophy.

## Wrap (Cornchip's Brother)
- **Silhouette:** A rounded, wrap/burrito-shaped body with comedic articulated legs, expressive eyes, and a mouth.
- **Role:** Cornchip's brother and the source of each level's obstacles — thrown pun ingredients ("mild" salsa, vegetarian cheese, etc.). Confirmed as the game's final boss, fought after all other levels' ingredients are collected (each level has its own boss guarding that level's ingredient; see "Level Bosses" below).
- **Narrative Function:** Estranged from Cornchip after an argument. Reconciliation occurs once Cornchip has collected all 7 ingredients and presents them to make "the ultimate crunch wrap."
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Proud and stubborn on the surface; the obstacle-throwing reads as wounded-sibling antics rather than genuine villainy, and he wants to reconcile as much as Cornchip does.

## Cheeto (Post-MVP)
- **Role:** Cornchip's best friend; planned second playable character for a future local co-op mode.
- **Status:** Concept only — no visual design or mechanics required for MVP.

## Level Bosses
Each level culminates in a boss battle guarding that level's ingredient; Wrap is the final boss, fought after all other levels' ingredients are collected (see `game-brief.txt` Level & Narrative Structure).

### Hot Sauce
- **Silhouette:** A happy squeeze bottle whose cap doubles as a sombrero; has cartoon legs and a face.
- **Role:** Level boss. Fires sauce projectiles from the opening in his hat, styled visually like lava — escalating the "mild salsa" joke from the base game brief.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Fiery, enthusiastic showoff.

### Avocado
- **Silhouette:** A dancing avocado.
- **Role:** Level boss. Dances and jumps rhythmically; each landing drops a puddle of guacamole that Cornchip cannot touch — a hazard-terrain attack rather than a direct projectile.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Groovy, carefree, always mid-dance.

### Salsa Bowl
- **Silhouette:** A rolling bowl of salsa, mounted on small corn-shaped wheels.
- **Role:** Fires tomato chunks at Cornchip while rolling around.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Not yet specified.
- **Open question:** Is Salsa Bowl a full level boss (like Hot Sauce/Avocado) or a regular enemy encountered within a level?

## Regular Enemies
Smaller, one-hit threats encountered within a level rather than guarding its ingredient. Unlike bosses, they don't patrol as far, don't attack at range, and go down in a single stomp.

### Cheese
- **Silhouette:** Not yet specified — currently uses placeholder-generation art matching the general "food character with cartoon legs and a face" template.
- **Role:** Regular enemy (resolved from the earlier boss-vs-enemy open question). Design brief describes his touch putting Cornchip to sleep for 10 seconds; the current build simplifies this to the same stumble-and-respawn reaction as any other hit, rather than a distinct sleep/incapacitation status effect — a real gap from the original idea, flagged here rather than silently dropped. Building a proper "sleep" effect (temporarily disabling input for a duration) is a good candidate for later polish.
- **Personality (draft — pending confirmation):** Not yet specified.
